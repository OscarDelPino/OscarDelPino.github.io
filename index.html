About Oscar

I am Oscar Del Pino Mercado, an industrial engineer graduated from the Universidad Panamericana in Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico. My professional career has focused on the maintenance and sourcing of databases for the regional supply chain, covering both the United States and Mexico. Currently, I play a role in this field as a database professional at Sanmina.

My true passion lies in working with data and applying advanced data science techniques to enhance operational efficiency and generate strategic insights. I hold a specialization in data science from the Universidad Panamericana, where I have acquired and refined fundamental tools and knowledge that have fueled my desire for continuous growth in this discipline.

My curious approach to data has led me to continuously explore new ways of applying analysis to address a variety of business challenges. I consider myself a self-learner, constantly seeking and assimilating new knowledge that enriches my professional background. In my ongoing pursuit of academic excellence, I am currently enrolled in the "CS50 AI with Python" course from Harvard University at Edx, solidifying my understanding and skills in artificial intelligence.

Throughout my career, I have worked on projects aimed at improving the quality of information, generating valuable insights to support strategic decision-making processes. My experience in using tools like Python, specializing in libraries such as Pandas, Numpy, Mathplotlib, Plotly, TensorFlow, and scikit-learn, is complemented by skills in SQL, with a particular focus on MSS (Microsoft SQL Server). In my current role, I frequently apply this expertise, handling Stored Procedures, queries, and other fundamental aspects of database administration.

I am committed to continuous improvement and look forward to contributing my analytical and technical skills to address challenges and seize opportunities in the field of data science.
